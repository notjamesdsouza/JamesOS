BUSINESS CHALLENGE

The Problem

Several Flipkart product listings were experiencing low visibility and limited sales due to weak marketplace SEO, incomplete catalog information, inconsistent stock availability, and limited campaign focus.

  • Missing SEO optimized titles & keywords
  • Incomplete attributes and descriptions
  • Inventory inconsistencies
  • Stock synchronization through Vinculum required continuous monitoring
  • Limited campaign support for Hero SKUs

TRANSFORMATION

Before vs After Optimization

Before

  • Low listing visibility
  • Weak SEO
  • Incomplete catalog
  • Stock inconsistencies
  • ~₹50K/day revenue

After

  • SEO optimized listings
  • Improved catalog quality
  • API stock synchronization
  • Campaign support
  • ₹3–4L/day revenue

PROJECT HIGHLIGHTS

Key Contributions

Core initiatives that contributed to marketplace growth and operational efficiency throughout the project.

Marketplace SEO

Improved listing discoverability through optimized titles, keywords, product descriptions and structured content.

Catalog Quality

Enhanced product attributes, specifications and listing completeness to improve customer experience.

Inventory Management

Monitored stock synchronization through Vinculum to ensure accurate product availability.

Campaign Support

Supported Hero SKU campaigns to increase visibility and improve marketplace performance.

Performance Tracking

Monitored CTR, CVR, revenue and listing performance to guide optimization decisions.

Contributed to scaling daily revenue from approximately ₹50K to ₹3–4L while supporting ₹1Cr+ monthly revenue.
