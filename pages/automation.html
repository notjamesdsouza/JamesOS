Automating Product Image Link Management with Excel VBA

Designed an Excel VBA automation solution to streamline product image link management by organizing Dropbox image URLs, standardizing image sequences, and automating link arrangement for faster and more accurate catalog updates.

BUSINESS CHALLENGE

The Problem

During product branding updates, image URLs stored in Dropbox required frequent replacement across multiple SKUs. Updating these links manually was repetitive, time-consuming, and increased the risk of incorrect image placement.

  • Manual replacement of image links
  • Repeated copy-and-paste work
  • High possibility of human error
  • Difficult to manage multiple product images
  • No standardized image sequence
